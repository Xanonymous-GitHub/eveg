E-Veg

Objective

This is an online shopping WebApp prototype which compatible with mobile devices, retrieve information from a catalogue of products available in the shop, fill a shopping basket with items, record shipping and payment data and invoke the ordering process resulting in an invoice being generated. The main usability goal of the WebApp is that it should be equally easy to use regardless of whether it is used on a desktop computer or on a mobile phone.
